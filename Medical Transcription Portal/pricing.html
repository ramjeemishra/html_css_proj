FAQ

Pricing Questions Answered

How is audio duration measured?
Duration is measured in the total playback time of the uploaded audio file, rounded up to the nearest minute for billing purposes.
When does the urgent multiplier apply?
The 2× urgent multiplier is applied when you select "Urgent" turnaround at upload time. You'll see the cost reflected in the real-time estimate before submitting.
Are there setup or subscription fees?
No. MediTranscribe is purely pay-per-use. You only pay for the audio minutes you transcribe. Monthly billing statements are issued automatically.
Do volume discounts apply automatically?
Yes. Volume pricing tiers are applied automatically each billing cycle based on cumulative monthly usage across your entire organization account.
Is the BAA included in all plans?
Yes, a Business Associate Agreement (BAA) is included with every hospital and enterprise account at no additional cost, as required for HIPAA compliance.
What payment methods are accepted?
We accept ACH bank transfer, credit card, and monthly purchase orders for enterprise accounts. Net-30 terms available for qualifying hospitals.
